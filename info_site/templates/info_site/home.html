Course Syllabus

Week 1: Getting Started with Technology

  • Introduction to digital technology
  • Understanding computers: hardware vs. software
  • Types of OS (e.g., Windows, macOS, Linux)
  • Factors to consider when selecting an OS for your device

Week 2: Navigating the Smartphone

  • Navigating computers or laptops
  • Navigating mobile devices
  • Setting up Wi-Fi and mobile data
  • Introduction to the Settings app and basic customization
  • Buying a digital device
  • Specifications to consider (e.g., processor, RAM, storage)

Week 3: Health, Safety & Troubleshooting

  • Health and safety while working with digital devices
  • Fixing problems with computers and mobile devices
  • Understanding device error messages and seeking support

Week 4: Internet Basics & Social Media for Beginners

  • Introduction to the Internet and the World Wide Web
  • Downloading and saving files
  • Safe browsing on the Internet
  • Understanding online safety
  • Creating accounts on social media platforms

Week 5: Accessibility and Safe Usage

  • Using accessibility features
  • Cybersecurity basics: Staying safe online
  • Strategies for dealing with cyberbullying
  • How to recognize scams, fraudulent emails, and security risks

Week 6: Getting Your Business Online / Understanding AI Technology

  • First steps to get your business online
  • Creating an account and setting up secure passwords
  • Introduction to AI and Generative AI
  • Being cautious about misinformation or deep fakes
