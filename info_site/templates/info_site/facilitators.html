Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need teaching experience?

Not necessarily! We're looking for patient, empathetic people. We'll provide training on teaching methods and curriculum. Tutoring, mentoring, or community service experience is a bonus but not required for supporting roles.

How much time commitment is required?

Lead Instructors: 6-8 hours/week (includes class time + prep + grading) Supporting Facilitators: 4-6 hours/week Social Media: 5-8 hours/week All positions are flexible and fit around your schedule.

When does the first cohort start?

Our first cohort is launching soon! Once we've hired our team, we'll begin recruiting students. If hired, we'll provide at least 2 weeks of training before classes begin.

Will I get a contract?

Yes! All hired facilitators will receive a written contract outlining responsibilities, compensation, and expectations. We want a professional, clear working relationship.

Can I apply for multiple roles?

Absolutely! Let us know which roles interest you most in the application. Some people work in multiple capacities if schedules allow.
